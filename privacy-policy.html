Privacy Policy for Skyguard

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 20.04.2024
  1. Introduction
    Welcome to Skyguard! Aerial Tech AG (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and related services.
  2. Information We Collect
    1. Information You Provide: This includes information required to create an account, such as your name, email address, and any optional information you provide such as a profile picture. It also includes any content you upload, such as drone flight plans and photos.
    2. Technical Information: We automatically collect certain information when you use our app, including your IP address, device type, operating system, and usage data such as how and when you use our app.
    3. Location Information: We collect precise geolocation data from your device if you permit the app to access device location to track drone flights.
  3. How We Use Your Information
    1. To Provide and Maintain Our Service: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve our services.
    2. To Communicate with You: We use your information to communicate with you about our services, including customer support and updates to our terms and policies.
    3. For Safety and Security: We use information about you to monitor for and prevent fraudulent activity, and to ensure the security of our services.
  4. Sharing Your Information
    1. With Service Providers: We share your information with third-party service providers that assist us in providing our services, such as cloud hosting providers.
    2. For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a subpoena or other legal request.
    3. With Your Consent: We may share your information with other parties where we have your explicit consent to do so.
  5. Your Rights
    You have certain rights regarding the information we hold about you. These may include the right to access, modify, or delete the personal information we have about you. You can usually exercise these rights through the settings and tools provided in our app.
  6. Data Retention
    We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
  7. International Transfers
    Your information may be transferred to, and maintained on, computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.
  8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
  9. Contact Us
    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@aerialte.ch.
